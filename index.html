## Profile I am a first year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin under the supervision of [Prof. Robert W. Heath Jr.](http://www.profheath.org/) and [Prof. Haris Vikalo](http://users.ece.utexas.edu/~hvikalo/index.html) and where I am a member of the at the Wireless Networking and Communications Group ([WNCG](https://wncg.org/)) . I am interested in the theory of problems in the intersection of machine learning, communications and sensing but with real world applications, particularly to autonomous systems. I Before, I completed my B.S. in Mathematics from [Universidad de Los Andes](https://uniandes.edu.co/en) in 2015 in Colombia. I worked on Monte Carlo methods for hypothesis testing on contingency tables under the supervision of [Prof. Mauricio Velasco](http://wwwprof.uniandes.edu.co/~mvelasco/Velasco.html). Afterwards, I had the chance to work with Google LA through the [Research for industrial Problems for Students](http://www.ipam.ucla.edu/programs/student-research-programs/research-in-industrial-projects-for-students-rips-2018/?tab=overview) at UCLA, and later as a data science researcher for industry and government projects at [Quantil](http://quantil.co/).
